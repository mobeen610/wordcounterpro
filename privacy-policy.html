Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we protect your data when using WordCounterPro.

1. Introduction

Welcome to WordCounterPro ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

By using WordCounterPro, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use our services.

Key Privacy Promise

Your text never leaves your browser. All word counting and text analysis happens locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to the text you enter into WordCounterPro.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We DO NOT collect:

  • Your text content: Any text you paste or type into WordCounterPro remains entirely on your device
  • Personal information: We don't require registration, so we don't collect names, emails, or contact information
  • Location data: We don't track your geographical location
  • Device information: We don't collect device identifiers, IP addresses (except for basic analytics), or browser fingerprints
  • Usage patterns: We don't monitor how you use the tool beyond basic anonymous analytics

The text processing happens entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. No text data is sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

3. Information We Collect (Minimal Analytics)

Anonymous Usage Data

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about website usage. This helps us understand how people use WordCounterPro and improve the service. This data includes:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Country of origin (at country level, not specific location)

This data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Cookies

We use cookies only for:

  • Google Analytics (to distinguish users)
  • Basic functionality (if we implement preferences in the future)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some website functionality.

4. How We Use Information

The limited anonymous data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Improving WordCounterPro's features and usability
  • Understanding which features are most valuable to users
  • Monitoring website performance and uptime
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Making decisions about future development

We never sell, rent, or trade your data with third parties. We never use your data for targeted advertising or marketing.

5. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with WordCounterPro. Google Analytics collects anonymous data as described above. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Font Awesome

We use Font Awesome for icons. This service may collect limited usage data. Learn more at https://fontawesome.com/privacy.

CDN Services

We use content delivery networks (CDNs) to serve CSS and JavaScript files faster. These services may collect basic connection data.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the limited data we collect. However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Since we don't collect or store your text content, there's no risk of your writing being exposed through our systems.

7. Children's Privacy

WordCounterPro is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Our tool is particularly popular with students, including those under 13, which is why we designed it with maximum privacy protection from the start.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Via our website contact form (when available)

  • By email (if provided in the future)
