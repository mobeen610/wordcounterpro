Our Story

WordCounterPro was born from a simple observation: writers, students, and professionals everywhere struggle with word counting. Whether it's meeting essay requirements, optimizing content for SEO, or staying within social media limits, everyone needs accurate word counting tools.

Founded in 2024, we set out to create the perfect word counter - one that's completely free, respects user privacy, and provides all the features writers need. Unlike other tools that require registration, show intrusive ads, or sell user data, WordCounterPro works entirely in your browser with no strings attached.

Today, we serve thousands of users worldwide, helping them write better, faster, and more effectively within their required constraints.
